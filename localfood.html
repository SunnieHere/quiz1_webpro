Local Foods from Cirebon

Dishes that are popular from Cirebon

Empal Gentong

The name of this dish comes from its method of preparation, which involves cooking it in a clay pot or earthenware. With a thick, yellowish broth rich in spices, Empal Gentong offers a savory and delicious flavor that is truly appetizing . A savory beef soup cooked in a clay pot with coconut milk and spices.

Nasi Jamblang

Nasi Jamblang is one of Cirebon's most iconic dishes, famous for its unique presentation using teak leaves to wrap the rice.

Mie Koclok

Mie Koclok is a distinctive Cirebon culinary specialty, known for its thick, savory, and white-colored broth. This dish differs from Bandung’s Mie Kocok, as it uses a combination of coconut milk and chicken stock, giving it a unique flavor.

Tahu Gejrot

Tahu Gejrot is one of Cirebon’s most famous and beloved snacks. It consists of pieces of fried tofu served with a fresh, sweet, sour, and spicy sauce..
