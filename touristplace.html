Tourist Places in Cirebon

Place to go if you're bored in Cirebon

Ciremai Mountain

Mount Ciremai is a prominent symmetrical stratovolcano in Cirebon, perfect for hiking and nature exploration.

Cirebon City Hall

Cirebon City Hall is the central government office of Cirebon, famous for its Dutch-style Art Deco architecture. A landmark colonial-style building that represents the city's history.

Taman Sari Gua Sunyaragi

Taman Sari Gua Sunyaragi is a cultural heritage complex and historical tourist site located in the city of Cirebon. An iconic water palace and cave complex with unique architecture and culture.

Keraton Kasepuhan

Keraton Kasepuhan is one of the oldest palaces in Cirebon, serving as the center of government and the residence of the Sultan of Cirebon, showcasing royal artifacts and traditions.

Keraton Kacirebonan

Keraton Kacirebonan is the youngest of the four palaces in Cirebon and is an important historical site that serves as a popular tourist destination.

Keraton Kanoman

Keraton Kanoman is one of the two main palaces in the Cirebon Sultanate, established in the 17th century following the division of the Cirebon Sultanate. Another royal palace of Cirebon, still preserving cultural heritage and ceremonies.
